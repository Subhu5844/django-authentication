Welcome

This is a minimal UI to test your cookie-based auth flow.

  1. Register with email & password → you’ll receive an OTP (check SMTP or console).
  2. Verify with your email + the OTP.
  3. Login with email + password (server sets auth_token HttpOnly cookie).
  4. Open “My Details” to hit /api/me/ (must be authenticated).
  5. Logout to clear the cookie + invalidate the server token.
